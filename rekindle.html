Rekindle was my final capstone proposal chosen out of 23 students to be one of the five final group projects. Rekindle is an app for couples looking to strengthen their relationship through rediscovery, introspection, and fun.

For this project, I was responsible for the initial conception, team selection and management, and SCRUM board and project management.

I also handled majority of the front end design and graphics, backend API for the Prompt and Discuss pages, as well as the quiz feature.
